TO DO LIST

How to use it:

  1. 1. First, click on "Start a New List"
  2. 2. Click on "Enter Tasks Here", type in your task and then click on the "Add" button.
  3. Alternatively, press the "Enter" key to add your item to the list
  4. 3. Completed tasks can be crossed off by simply clicking on them
  5. 4. To remove a task, just click on the "x" button
